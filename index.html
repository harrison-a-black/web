05About

Designer.
Strategist.
Sunshine Coast.

Web Design Visual Identity Digital Design Brand Strategy Figma HTML / CSS

I'm Harrison — a freelance designer and digital strategist based on the Sunshine Coast, working with organisations that exist for something bigger than the bottom line.

My background sits at the intersection of design and communication. I've worked with schools, disability service providers, and purpose-led organisations — helping them look and communicate as good as the work they actually do.

I believe good design is never decoration. It's the clearest expression of what you value, who you're for, and why it matters. When that's done right, the right people find you — and trust you faster.

I work with a small number of clients at a time so I can give each project the attention it deserves. If that sounds like the kind of working relationship you're after, I'd love to hear from you.
